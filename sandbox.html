Note about double-sided 12" records
Double-sided 12" records longer than roughly 10 minutes per side take significantly longer to cut and are not practical for larger runs at this pricing. For this configuration the order form is limited to 5 units. If you're planning a larger project please feel free to contact Crosstalk Records and we'll happily advise on the best approach.
